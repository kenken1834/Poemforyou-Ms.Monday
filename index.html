The Women You Are

Your kindness shines like flowers in bloom,
A fragrance so captivating, it fills the room.
With ever hue you create,
You light up the world with that shining face.

The beauty you share, so bold and fine,
A beacon of warmth, a guiding light.
In moments of doubt, you make it high,
With strength and courage, reaching the sky.

Your presence, a gift, so rare and true,
We thank the stars for someone like you.
For all that you are and all that you do,
This poem, Ms. Monday, is just for you.
I really like you, Monday.
